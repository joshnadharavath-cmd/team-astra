01 — About Us

Who We Are

Team Astra is the premier multidisciplinary aerial robotics team of IIITDM Kancheepuram, operating under the SAEINDIA Collegiate Club to push the boundaries of Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) technology.

Comprising a dedicated group of students from mechanical, electronics, and computer science backgrounds, the team focuses on the end-to-end design, fabrication, and autonomous programming of advanced drones.

From securing top national rankings in prestigious competitions like the ISRO Robotics Challenge and SAE AeroTHON to hosting hands-on technical workshops, Team Astra serves as a vital innovation hub that bridges the gap between theoretical engineering and real-world aerospace applications.

Their work emphasizes cutting-edge research in autonomous navigation, structural optimization, and intelligent flight systems, solidifying their reputation as one of the leading student robotics collectives in India.

03 — Domains

What We Do

Structural Design

Airframe design, FEA analysis, composite material selection, and aerodynamic optimization for maximum flight efficiency.

Electronics & Avionics

Flight controller integration, sensor fusion, power distribution systems, and custom PCB design for reliable UAV operation.

Autonomous Systems

ROS-based navigation, computer vision, path planning algorithms, and GPS-denied autonomous flight capabilities.
